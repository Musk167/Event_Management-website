Event Countdown

Riviera

Riviera is the annual cultural festival of VIT University, Vellore. The festival attracts a large number of students from across the country who participate in various cultural and artistic events. Riviera features a wide range of activities, including music, dance, drama, art, and literature. The festival also includes fun activities, food stalls, and celebrity performances. Riviera provides a platform for students to showcase their talents, learn from experts, and create lasting memories.

graVITas VIT

graVITas is the annual techno-management festival of VIT Vellore that provides a platform for students from all over India to showcase their skills and creativity. The festival features a range of activities, including technical workshops, guest lectures, exhibitions, cultural events, and a startup expo. With a changing theme every year, graVITas is an exciting and enriching experience for students who want to explore the world of technology and innovation.

Clubs and chapters

VIT Vellore has a diverse range of clubs and chapters, including music, drama, entrepreneurship, photography, robotics, literary, TEDxVIT, programming, and sports. These clubs provide a platform for students to pursue their interests, develop new skills, and interact with like-minded individuals through various events and activities.
